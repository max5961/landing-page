This website is about monkeys
A monkey is a primate of the Haplorrhini suborder and simian infraorder. Monkeys are generally considered to be intelligent.
Monkey Facts
The pygmy marmoset is the smallest known monkey in the world.
The largest monkey in the world is the mandrill.
Spider monkeys are the most acrobatic of all monkeys.
A troop refers to a group of monkeys.
